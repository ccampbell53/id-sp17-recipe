Jiffy

Cornbread

Casserole

Total Time:
2 hr
Cooking Time:
1 hr 40 min
Yield:
10 servings
Level:
Easy
Ingredients / Directions

Things you will need:

  • 1 can whole corn (17 oz)
  • 1 can creamed corn (17 oz)
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • ½ cup margarine melted
  • 1 package Jiffy Corn Muffin mix (8½ oz)
  • 2 eggs

How to make it:

  1. Preheat oven to 300°F.
  2. Drain can of whole corn. (not creamed!)
  3. Combine all ingredients into a large bowl.
  4. Mix thoroughly.
  5. Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish.
  6. Bake for 1 hour and 40 minutes.
